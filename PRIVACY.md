# Privacy Policy for SilentMode

**Last updated: June 2025**

## Information We Collect
- Email address (for authentication)
- Tone preference, text length preference
- VIP contact list, custom mode settings

## Information We Process But NEVER Store
- Message content (processed in real-time, immediately discarded)
- Sender information, voice note audio

## Third-Party Services
- Firebase (authentication, database)
- GROQ (AI processing - does not store inputs)
- HuggingFace (voice transcription - does not store data)
- Vercel (API hosting)
